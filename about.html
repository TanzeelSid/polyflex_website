About Polyflex

Established in 1983, Polyflex Printing Services grew from a custom printing shop into a full-service packaging partner. We focus on quality, customer-centric solutions, and sustainability.

1983 — Founded

Started as a custom printing operation focused on local clients.

2000 — Automated Label Printing

Expanded capabilities to include automated label printing and plastic packaging.

2015 — ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO quality standards and food-safe packaging processes.

2023 — Started Exports

Began exporting packaging products to international markets.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To be recognized as a leading global packaging partner, known for excellence, sustainability, and innovation.

Mission: To deliver innovative, reliable, and sustainable packaging solutions that protect products, empower brands, and create value for our clients.
